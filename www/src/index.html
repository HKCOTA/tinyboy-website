Open Design 3D Printer for Education

Cheap , Tiny , Cute , Easy to build

Less is more

The physical size of the printer is just 15x15x22cm. It prints object as most in 8.5 * 8.5 * 8.5cm. The gain from the trade off is the cost down of material and maintenance. Take out components for making large printed object, the design become simple and lean. It make it as a cheap 3D printer solution

Small Amount Production

The body is constructed by acrylic planes with the help of laser cutter. The manufacturing process is easy, cheap and fast once you have a laser cutter. In fact , the price of an A5 laser cutter can be as cheap as USD $400. That make the cost of small amount production be reasonably low.

The expected material cost to produce one set of printer should be around USD $130. It can be even lower in large amount production.

Build a Printer by Student

Tinyboy is designed for easy to build. The assemble from kit can be done by just using a screw driver. We wish students can make their own machine and maintain by themselves. Then they become a pioneer whose may make for other people. So that the use of 3D printer can be spread out to more student.

Classroom Friendly

A 3D printer usually take 20~30 minutes to print a meaningful object. Suppose there has 30 students in a class, how many student can put their design into real in a lesson?

Tinyboy solve this problem by make it cheap and small. It may purchase / build multiple Tinyboy for a classroom so that more student can enjoy the creative process.
