Open Design 3D Printer for Education

Cheap , Tiny , Cute , Easy to build

Less is more

Physical size of Tinyboy is scaled down to just 15 * 15 * 22cm. It can print object in 8.5 * 8.5 * 8.5cm the largest. Taking away components for printing large object, the printer’s design becomes simple and lean. At the same time its costs for both production and maintenance are lowered, which make it a cheap 3D printer solution.

Small Amount Production

The body is constructed by acrylic planes with the help of laser cutter. The manufacturing process is easy, cheap and fast once you have a laser cutter. In fact , the price of an A5 laser cutter can be as cheap as USD $400. That make the cost of small amount production be reasonably low.

The expected material cost to produce one set of printer should be around USD $130. It can be even lower in large amount production.

Built by Students

Tinyboy is designed for “easy to build”. Assembling from the kit can be done by using just a screw driver. We wish students can make and maintain their own machines by themselves, or even become a pioneer to produce more for others. Popularity of both the printer and 3D printing can then be spread from students to the community.

Classroom Friendly

A 3D printer usually takes 20~30 minutes to print an object. With a normal class size of 30 and lesson duration being 35-40 minutes in Hong Kong’s schools, how much money and storage space should a school invest on 3D printers - if it only wishes students can put their design into real within a lesson?

Tinyboy solves this problem by making it cheap and small. School may purchase / build multiple Tinyboy for a classroom so that more student can enjoy the creative process.
